How to Perform Eigendecomposition
• Q: How can we actually perform eigendecomposition?
• Two popular approaches: QR algorithm and power iteration.
   • QR algorithm is a standard way but slow for large matrices.
   • Power iteration is fast but finds only the largest eigenvalues.
• We’ll focus on power iteration as we assume a large matrix.

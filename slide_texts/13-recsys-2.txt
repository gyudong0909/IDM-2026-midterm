Differences from SVD
• UV decomposition is mainly an empirical approach.
  • 𝑈 and 𝑉 need not to be orthonormal matrices.
  • Relies on stochastic optimization, not a closed-form solution.
• UV decomposition seeks generalization to unseen data.
  • UV ignores the missing entries, not treating them as zero.
  • UV minimizes RMSE on only the training portion.
  • Larger 𝑘 is not necessarily better (may increase test RMSE).

Best Low-Rank Approximation
• Given 𝑟 < rank 𝑀 , SVD gives the best axes to project on.
   • In other words, it gives the best rank-𝒓 approximation of the matrix.
   • That minimizes the reconstruction error 𝑀 − 𝐵 $ .
• Intuitive reason:
   • 𝜎% represents how much “energy” the matrix has along 𝑣% .
   • Truncation keeps the top 𝑟 orthogonal “energy directions.”
   • As a result, SVD can minimize the loss of energy.

Determining the Low Rank
